Day 17 - Sunday 26th Nov

Drive from Murchison to Picton(116 miles) and ferry to Wellington

A leisurely breakfast before setting off on the road for the last time. Much of the trip to Picton is on State Highway 63. This is normally a quiet back road but since the recent earthquake blocked Highway 1 north of Christchurch this is now part of the major detour. Hopefully the repairs to Highway 1 will be complete by the time of our trip but we'll need to keep an eye on this in case we need to allow more time.
We'll drop the car off at the Picton Ferry terminal before boarding the Interislander Ferry destined for Wellington on the North Island. Check in is by 1330 for a departure at 1415 on Kaitaki with arrival into Wellington at 1745. For extra comfort and free food & booze we'll book seats in Kaitaki Plus Lounge.
Either a shuttle or a taxi will get us to our central hotel in Wellington - Rydges.
