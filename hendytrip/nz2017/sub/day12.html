Day 12 - Tuesday 21st Nov

Scenic drive from Christchurch to Queenstown - 323 miles

Time to get on the road for an all day scenic drive to Queenstown. Be prepared for an early start and some excellent sightseeing.

The route takes in Rakaia Gorge, Lake Tekapo, Lake Pukaki with good views of Mount Cook, Lindis Pass and Cromwell close to Bannockburn.

Burkes Brewing is reputedly New Zealand's smallest brewery and can be found on our route in a place called Twizel.
Our accommodation in Queenstown is a Deluxe King Room at the Coronation Lodge which is within easy staggering distance of the downtown bars and restaurants.

Best not stay up too late because we'll have to be out early the next morning.
