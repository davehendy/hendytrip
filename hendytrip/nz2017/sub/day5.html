Day 5 - Tuesday 14th Nov

Overnight flight from Hong Kong to Sydney

Our flight to Sydney departs in the evening so we'll take a leisurely breakfast before checking out from the hotel by midday. We'll take the complimentary K3 shuttle bus from the hotel which leaves every 12 minutes for Kowloon MTR station where we can check in our bags for the flight.

After checking in our bags we can spend more time in Hong Kong before returning at our leisure to the MTR station for the Airport Express train.

There are a number of lounges at Hong Kong airport where we can relax while awaiting Cathay Pacific 9 hour 20 minute overnight 777 flight BA4137/CX161 departing at 2125 for Sydney.
Our seats are 15A/16A in Business Class. Dinner will be served after take off before we snuggle down for a few hours sleep in our beds. Breakfast is served before arrival into Sydney.
