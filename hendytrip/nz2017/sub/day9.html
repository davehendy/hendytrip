Day 9 - Saturday 18th Nov

Travel from Sydney to Christchurch, New Zealand

Our flight to Christchurch was going to leave Sydney in the morning but it's now been changed to the evening. Hotel checkout time is 1600 so I'd suggest a leisurely breakfast, a final stroll around Sydney and then a taxi or train to the airport where we can relax in the Qantas Business Lounge. No need to rush though so maybe we could try the Airport Link train. This schedule change is a real pain in the arse but Intercontinental Ambassador membership allows for a late check out and that helps.
The flight is Business Class on QF139/BA7423 Qantas Jetconnect and departs at 1845. We have seats 2D/2F and will be served food on board.
Flight time to Christchurch is 3 hours and 10 minutes and is scheduled to arrive at 2355 local time which is 11 hours ahead of the UK.
Once through immigration we pick up our Avis car. They know what flight we're on so they should have the car ready for us. Nothing fancy, just something like a Toyota Corolla hatchback that'll do us for a week or so.
We're staying in the centre of Christchurch at Fino Hotel & Suites. We have a one bedroom deluxe suite which even comes with a washing machine. With the flight schedule change it'll likely be 0100 or later by the time our heads hit the pillow. Such is the life of jet-setters, eh?
