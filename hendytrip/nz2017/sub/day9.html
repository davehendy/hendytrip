Day 9 - Saturday 18th Nov

Travel from Sydney to Christchurch, New Zealand

I'd suggest an early checkout at about 0730 from the hotel & taxi to the airport where we can get breakfast in the Qantas Business Lounge.
The flight is Business Class on QF139/BA7423 Qantas Jetconnect and departs at 1055. We have seats 2D/2F and will be served lunch on board.
Flight time to Christchurch is 3 hours and 10 minutes and is scheduled to arrive at 1605 local time which is 13 hours ahead of the UK.
Once through immigration we pick up our Avis car. Nothing fancy, just something like a Toyota Corolla hatchback that'll do us for a week or so.
We're staying in the centre of Christchurch at Fino Hotel & Suites. We have a one bedroom deluxe suite which even comes with a washing machine.
