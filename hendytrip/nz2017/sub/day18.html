Day 18 - Monday 27th Nov

Wellington

We have two nights in Wellington so today is our chance to take in what the New Zealand capital has to offer. Daytime temperatures will be on the up and we can expect something around 20C with good chances of sun & wind.
Not far from the hotel is the Wellington Cable Car which runs up the hill to the Botanic Garden. A good place to stretch the legs and take in the views.
Wellington offers an excellent range of brewpubs in the centre. There's Mac's BrewBar or there's Black Dog Brewery or maybe Parrotdog Brewery or even Fork & Brewer.
Other craft beer bars are Malt House, Little Beer Quarter, Brühaus, Hashigo Zake, Golding's Free Dive, Rogue & Vagabond.
All that in one day? It'll take some doing.
