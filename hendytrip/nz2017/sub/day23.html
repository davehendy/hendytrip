Day 23 - Saturday 2nd" Dec

Auckland to Singapore via Sydney

It's time to start on the homeward journey by way of a few nights in Singapore. Travelling BA First Class will certainly help.
Our trip today is in two stages. The first leg takes us to Sydney airport and from there we connect for the flight to Singapore. Total journey time is 18 hours including a few hours enjoying the First Class lounge at Sydney.
The airport terminal is just across the road from the Novotel. Once we've checked in our bags through to Singapore we can grab some breakfast either at the hotel or in the lounge - or both.
Our first flight today is BA7418/QF142 departing for Sydney at 0835. Flight time is 3 hours 40 minutes. We're in Business Class seats 2A/2C where more breakfast will be served.
On arrival into Sydney at 1015 we make our way through flight connections to the famous and well-regarded Qantas First Class Lounge. We'll have a few hours to enjoy here. Spa treatment, food & drink are all available while we relax in comfort.
We're back on British Airways for the journey from Sydney to Singapore. BA16 is a Boeing 777-300 departing at 1630 and we're up the front in First Class seats 2K/3K. It's an 8 hour flight including dinner and gets us to Singapore at 2130 (8 hours ahead of the UK).
On arrival at Singapore we'll hop into a taxi and get straight to our Superior room at the Clarke Quay Novotel. That'll be the end of a long day so let's hope the bed is comfy.
