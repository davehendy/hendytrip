Day 1 - Friday 10th Nov

Overnight trip from Heathrow to Hong Kong

Travel to Heathrow T5 around lunch time before retiring to the BA lounge to await our 12 hour BA A380 flight BA31 departing for Hong King at 1825.

We have Club World (Business Class) seats 53J/53K on the upper deck. There will be an evening meal on board before getting the chance for a few hours sleep in our lie-flat beds. Breakfast is served in the morning prior to arrival in Hong Kong.
