Day 15 - Friday 24th Nov

Drive from Queenstown to Franz Joseph (220 miles)

This should be a spectacular drive as we soon head north on the Crown Range Road towards Wanaka. Lakes Hawea and Wanaka should offer some interesting views on our route as should Fox Glacier.
Franz Joseph Glacier sits above the small town of Franz Joseph where we stop for a night at the Alpine Glacier Motel in a King Studio. The glacier is some 5km south of the town so we'll drive down and have a look.
Probably worth having a look at the Snakebite Brewery or the Alice May pub.
