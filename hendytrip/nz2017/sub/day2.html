Day 2 - Saturday 11th Nov

Arrival into Hong Kong

BA31 is due to arrive in Hong Kong at 1420 local time (7 hours ahead of UK).

After picking up our bags we'll proceed to counter B17 in arrivals hall B where we check in for our shuttle transfer to the Intercontinental Grand Stanford Hotel at Tsim Sha Tsui, Kowloon.

We're in a Club Premier room which entitles us to a bunch of extras such as free breakfast & booze in the Club Lounge.
