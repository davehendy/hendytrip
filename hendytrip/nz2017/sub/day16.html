Day 16 - Saturday 25th Nov

Drive from Franz Joseph to Murchison (225 miles)

No time for hanging about. This is the busiest part of the trip as we head further north up the coastal road. A prompt start after breakfast should give us plenty of time to enjoy the sights on the way.
We'll be passing through the old gold rush town of Hokitika as we head towards Greymouth, the home of Monteith's brewery.
Then onward to Punakaiki and the Pancake Rocks before turning inland ready for tomorrow's final car trip on the South Island.
We have a one night stay in the small town of Murchison. Our accommodation is centrally located at Murchison Motels in a King Studio.
