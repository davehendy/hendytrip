Day 20 - Wednesday 29th Nov

Auckland

The first of two and a half days in Auckland. This time of year is reputed to be sunny and pleasant with temperatures up to 20C.
New Zealand's largest city is built on 50 volcanos and not all of them are extinct, although the last eruption was 600 years ago.
Any tall building invites an ascent and in this case the Sky Tower awaits us.
Auckland offers refreshment at a number of brewpubs. Try Brothers Beer or Shakespeare Hotel & Brewery. Other places for beer include Brew on Quay and Brewers Co-operative and 16 Tun or maybe Vulture's Lane.
