Day 3 - Sunday 12th Nov

Hong Kong

November is a good time of year to visit Hong Kong. Average high is 24C and average low is 19C with very little rain. The skies are mostly blue and humidity is reduced.

Getting around Hong Kong looks like being no problem at all and this link gives a good overview. The Sold Tourist Octopus Card costs HK$39 and looks to be the thing to get.
A trip over to Hong Kong Island on the Star Ferry has to be part of the trip, especially since there's some beer to be sampled there. Try Tipping Point Brewing or Craft Brew & Co or maybe Roundhouse Tap Room or possibly Craft Cow or even Little Creatures Brewing.
