Day 7 - Thursday 16th Nov

Sydney sightseeing

November marks the end of Spring in Sydney with maximum temperatures in the low 20s while overnight minimums are in the high teens. There should be plenty of sunshine with the chance of a spot or two of rain.

Our hotel is handily placed for the Opera House, The Rocks and Harbour Bridge so there'll be plenty to do.

Getting around Sydney on public transport trains, buses & ferries is simple enough. Probably best to get an Opal Cardto make life simple.

Needless to say there's plenty of beer available in Sydney. Some establishments of interest might include Lord Nelson Brewery and King St. Brewhouse. Also nearby is Sydney's oldest pub Fortune of War.
